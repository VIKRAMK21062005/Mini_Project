Best Selling book by our Alumnus

Feburary 28, 2022

SARC BIT Mesra is pleased to congratulate Yashaswee Raman for his latest bestselling book, 'Test of Times: Reliving Indian Cricket History' that dives into the sea of nostalgia and takes us on a beautiful ride of the history of Indian cricket. Yashaswee Raman studied B.Pharm from BIT Mesra where he passed out in 2020. He has always been a cricket fanatic and he was usually spotted doing commentary during sports fests.
